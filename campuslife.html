My Campus Life

My campus life at Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM) has been an exciting and transformative journey. As a second-year Bachelor of Information Technology student, I’ve had the chance to experience both academic and personal growth in a supportive environment.

Staying at the student hostel has taught me a lot about independence and responsibility. I’ve made great friends from different backgrounds, and we often help each other through studies, share meals, and enjoy campus activities together. It’s like having a second family away from home.

UTHM offers a peaceful and well-organized campus. The facilities—like the libraries, computer labs, lecture halls, and outdoor spaces—make learning and relaxing equally enjoyable. I especially like walking around the campus in the evening. The greenery and calm atmosphere help me recharge after a busy day.
